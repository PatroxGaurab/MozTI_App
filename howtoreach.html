How To Reach TO BESU

Reaching To BESU:

From Rabindra Sadan :
1. Shalimar-Shealdah mini bus
2. MS-35
3. By K-7 or any bus going to Andul get down to College Ghat and walk 5 minutes to reach BESU
Bus takes normally 15 to 20 minutes to reach BESU.
You can reach BESU from Howrah Railway Station by Bus no.55 or B.Garden Mini bus via Shalimar.
Bus takes normally 25 to 30 minutes to reach BESU.
From Shealdah you can take B.Garden Mini Bus to reach BESU. It comes via EXIDE Rabindra Sadan.
From Dumdum Airport you can reach BESU either via Howrah Railway Station or via Rabindra Sadan. To reach Howrah Railway Station from Dumdum Airport you may take bus no. L238 or bus no. V2 .
Otherwise you can come to Dumdum Metro from the Airport by bus 30B or bus DN9/1. From Dumdum you may take metro rail to reach Rabindra Sadan.
All the above buses are quite frequent
